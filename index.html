The Cyber Swaraj Project

Polycentric Models of Governance for Cybersecurity

Swaraj is a Gandhian term that means self-governance. Cyber Swaraj refers to the ability of interested stakeholders to come together to build polycentric models of governance to provision cybersecurity. The Cyber Swaraj Project aims to analyze the design of these institutions using Elinor Ostrom's Institutiaonl Analysis and Development (IAD) framework, colloquially known as the Common-Pool Resources (CPR) framework to support sustainable cybersecurity.
